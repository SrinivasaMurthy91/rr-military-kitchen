Our Story

RR Military Kitchen started as a cloud kitchen serving authentic Andhra and South Indian food through WhatsApp orders. Our spices are sourced from India and every dish is prepared with traditional recipes. Our vision is to expand into a premium restaurant brand, social media, YouTube and multiple UK locations.
